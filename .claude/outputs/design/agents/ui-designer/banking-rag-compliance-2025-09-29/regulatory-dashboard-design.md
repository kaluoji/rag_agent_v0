## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Design Philosophy
The regulatory dashboard system follows a **data-first, action-oriented** approach, prioritizing critical compliance information while maintaining visual clarity. The design emphasizes:
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Show essential metrics first, details on demand
- **Contextual Intelligence**: Surface relevant insights based on user role and current focus
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Use size, color, and position to guide attention to critical items
- **Actionable Insights**: Every metric should lead to a clear next step

### 1.3 Visual Language
- **Color Psychology**: Red for critical, amber for warnings, green for compliance
- **Information Density**: 5-7 primary metrics per view (Miller's Law)
- **Refresh Rates**: Real-time for critical, 5-min for important, hourly for background
- **Interaction Patterns**: Click for details, hover for context, drag for customization
